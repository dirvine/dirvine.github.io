Careers with MaidSafe

MaidSafe strives to create a culture where team members spend their day doing what they want to do.

We see no point in coming to “work” and doing stuff that you don’t love.

That’s why it’s important to us that you do what’s important to you.

Our job is to provide an environment where you can grow not just as an employee but where you can grow your skills, your abilities, your confidence and ultimately your potential.

Your job is simply to be of value to the team.

MaidSafe is essentially a deep technology company. The majority of our team are deep technical thinkers. But this doesn’t mean you have to be fluent in C++. What is of the utmost importance to us is that you are fluent in the ability to communicate ideas and solutions, whether you are an engineer or an accountant.

As much as possible we operate a flat structure. There is a board, but there are no “bosses”. Your progress and successes will be measured by the team and we expect you, as part of the team, to measure our progress and successes.

MaidSafe is looking for great people to join a great team. If you are great and want to be part of a movement that is shaping the future of technology, click here to send us your CV.
